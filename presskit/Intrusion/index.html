Description

Intrusion is a science fiction visual novel in which you take the role of an investigator in a reality where everything has to be done quickly. In your job, you need to get inside the minds of suspects to reveal their secrets and find out whether they are guilty or innocent. But beware, hasty judgments can have drastic consequences, as can taking too long to make decisions.

Features

  • All characters animated
  • Multiple choices and endings
  • A story full of plot twists
  • Dynamic visual novel
  • Captivating and interesting characters
